# Topor Inspired Prompts

## Full Prompts
A man using his own ribs as the rungs of a ladder.

A dinner plate where the food is screaming back at the diner.

A woman unzipping her skin to reveal a second, identical woman.

A cityscape where the buildings are made of giant, stacked teeth.

A businessman’s necktie that turns into a constrictor snake.

A pair of eyes floating in a bowl of soup like meatballs.

A telephone handset that is a human ear on both ends.

A tree where the leaves are tiny, reaching hands.

A man trying to shave, but his reflection is refusing to move.

A bird’s nest made entirely of human hair and jewelry.

A suitcase that, when opened, contains a miniature, crowded theater.

A teapot pouring out thick, black shadows instead of tea.

A hand with a mouth in the palm, biting its own fingers.

A person’s shadow detaching itself to walk in the opposite direction.

A bouquet of flowers where the centers are staring eyeballs.

A man sewing his own shadow back onto his heels.

A clock where the hands are long, pointed fingers.

A face where the nose has been replaced by a smaller, screaming face.

A bathtub overflowing with letters that have never been read.

A person wearing a mask that is more realistic than their actual face.

A chair that has human legs and is trying to walk away.

A typewriter where the keys are individual human teeth.

A man looking into a mirror and seeing his internal organs.

A staircase that leads into a giant, open mouth.

A sky where the clouds are shaped like various internal organs.

A pair of scissors cutting through the fabric of the sky.

A person whose umbrella is raining on them from the inside.

A loaf of bread that contains a hidden, sleeping infant.

A man’s brain being sliced like a block of cheese.

A dollhouse where the dolls are performing a complex surgery.

A person’s footprints filling up with dark, murky water.

A lightbulb that emits darkness, turning the room pitch black.

A woman’s dress made entirely of living butterflies.

A man’s beard that has become a tangled web of spiders.

A window that looks out onto a different century.

A person whose reflection is several seconds behind their movements.

A musical instrument that produces visible, solid shapes instead of sound.

A dinner party where all the guests are wearing paper bags over their heads.

A man’s shadow being stepped on, causing him physical pain.

A bed that is actually a giant, open book.

A person’s thoughts escaping their head as colorful, toxic smoke.

A mirror that shows the viewer as they will look in fifty years.

A bird with human arms instead of wings.

A person whose skin is covered in delicate, intricate maps.

A fountain that is flowing with black ink.

A man trying to catch his own head as it rolls away.

A house where the doors and windows are constantly shifting positions.

A person whose veins are visible and arranged like a city map.

A giant eye watching a small, oblivious crowd from the horizon.

A man melting into a puddle, leaving only his hat and glasses behind.
